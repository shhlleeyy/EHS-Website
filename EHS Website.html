Mission

The Education High School commits itself to the noble task of participating in the evangelizing work of the Church. By providing students with a quality Catholic education, the school aims to instill the virtues of truth and love as espoused by the Angelic Doctor, St. Thomas Aquinas.

Vision

The Education High School seeks to evolve into one of the leading Catholic secondary institutions of learning in the country by strengthening and revitalizing the curriculum, updating the faculty, and networking with other institutions in the educational community.
